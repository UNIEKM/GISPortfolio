About

I have a strong passion for nature and enjoy exploring the natural world. My recreational interests encompass a variety of activities, such as golf, fishing, hiking, foraging, and snowboarding. Moreover, I have a keen appreciation for diverse cuisines, cultures, historical contexts, and artistic expressions. My interest in mycology has led me to gain knowledge in mushroom cultivation through self-directed learning, encompassing laboratory techniques, harvest management, and supply chain development with local restaurants. As someone who enjoys discovering new places and experiencing different cultures, I have had the opportunity to live and work in several vibrant cities such as Vancouver, Ottawa, Montreal, Prince Edward County, and Madrid. These experiences have broadened my perspective and increased my appreciation for diverse cultures and lifestyles.
