About Me

I have a deep love for mother nature and enjoy exploring and learning about the natural world. I spend my free time playing golf, fishing, hiking, foraging, and snowboarding. I also love spending time on the boat, relaxing on the beach, exploring food and culture, and appreciating history and art. In addition, I am fascinated with the world of mycology and have taught myself mushroom cultivation including lab techniques, harvest management, and sales to small restaurants.

As someone with a passion for exploring new places and cultures, I have been fortunate enough to live and work in several vibrant cities, including Vancouver, Ottawa, Montreal, Prince Edward County, and even Madrid. These experiences have broadened my horizons and instilled in me a deep appreciation for diversity and different ways of life.
